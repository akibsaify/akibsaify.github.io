## 1. Voltas 123V Adjustable Inverter — Best Budget 1 Ton

Voltas is India's #1 AC brand by market share — and for good reason. Their 1 Ton 3 Star inverter is the safest budget buy.

**Pros:**

- Wide voltage operation (160V–290V) — important in Delhi/UP

**Cons:**

**Room size**: Best for rooms 100–120 sq ft

## How to Choose Between 1 Ton and 1.5 Ton

**Buy 1 Ton if:**
- Room is 100–130 sq ft
- Room has 1–2 windows
- Ground floor or shaded room

**Buy 1.5 Ton if:**
- Room is 130–180 sq ft
- Direct sunlight on the room
- Top floor (heat absorption from roof)
- 3+ people regularly in the room

**Verdict**: 5-star pays off in 2–3 years if you use the AC 4+ months/year. If you only use it 2 months/year (like mild northern India), 3-star is fine.
